Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected when you use MemeMorphs and how it is used. MemeMorphs is designed to minimize personal data and rely mainly on on-chain wallet activity.

1. Wallet & On-Chain Data

When you connect a wallet, we see your public wallet address and on-chain actions that interact with MemeMorphs contracts. This data is public on the blockchain and cannot be made private by us.

2. Off-Chain Profile Data

If you create a MemeMorphs profile (display name, bio, avatar URL, etc.) this information is stored in our database to show it in the interface. You should not store sensitive personal data in your profile.

3. Analytics & Logs

We may use basic, privacy-friendly analytics or server logs to understand traffic (for example page views, error rates). These are used only to improve stability and user experience.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

Some information (for example your last connected wallet address or UI preferences) may be stored in your browser’s local storage or cookies. You can clear this data at any time from your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

MemeMorphs may integrate with external services such as NFT marketplaces, RPC providers, or image hosts. Your use of those services is governed by their own privacy policies.

6. Children

MemeMorphs is not directed to children under 18. If you believe we have stored data about a minor, contact us and we will review and remove it where possible.

7. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as the project evolves. When we do, we will update the “last updated” date on this page.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, requests or concerns contact: mememorph.digital.evolution@gmail.com.
