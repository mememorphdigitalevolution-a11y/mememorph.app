Project Rules & Community Guidelines

These rules define how explorers, creators and traders should behave inside the MemeMorphs ecosystem. They are here to protect the project, the community and the creative spirit of Digital Evolution.

1. Respect the Community

  • No hate speech, harassment, threats or discrimination.
  • No spam, scams, fake giveaways or phishing links.
  • Debate is welcome, personal attacks are not.

2. Fair Trading & Anti-Scam

  • Do not impersonate the team or pretend to be an official partner.
  • Always double-check contract addresses before signing any transaction.
  • Report suspicious activity to the moderators as soon as possible.

3. Content & NFTs

  • No illegal, explicit or hateful content inside MemeMorphs art, names or descriptions.
  • Do not upload content you do not have rights to (no stolen art, no copyright abuse).
  • MemeMorphs may hide or remove content that violates these rules.

4. Energy, Levels & Game Mechanics

Energy balances, XP and levels are part of an experimental game system. Abusing bugs, exploits or automation that harms other players or the economy is not allowed.

5. Fees & Revenue Share

The MemeMorphs team may set or update creator fees, platform fees or revenue splits for marketplace activity. Any official fee structure will be explained in the project documentation and announcements.

6. Enforcement

Breaking these rules may result in actions such as: muted or banned accounts in community channels, hiding of NFTs from the interface, or blocking access to certain features.

7. Evolving Project

As MemeMorphs grows, new rules may be added and existing ones may be updated. Important changes will be announced in the official channels (Discord, X, or the main website).

8. Contact & Appeals

If you believe a decision was unfair or you have questions about the rules, you can contact the team at mememorph.digital.evolution@gmail.com.
